Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

How far in advance should I book? +
We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance for smaller events and 4-6 weeks for large events like weddings. That said, we do our best to accommodate last-minute requests.
Do you serve outside Jakarta? +
Yes! We currently serve Jakarta, Bogor, Depok, Tangerang, and Bekasi (Jabodetabek). We are expanding to other cities — drop us a message to check availability in your area.
Can I customize the menu? +
Absolutely. We love working with clients to create a menu that fits their taste, dietary needs, and budget. Just mention it in your quote request.
What is included in the catering package? +
Our packages typically include food preparation, delivery, setup, serving equipment, and cleanup. Specific inclusions depend on the package you choose.
Is there a minimum order? +
Our minimum is 30 guests. For smaller gatherings, we offer a curated drop-off service — ask us about it when you request a quote.
